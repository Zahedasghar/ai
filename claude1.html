Understanding Limitations

Knowledge Cutoff

  • Training data goes through January 2025
  • For very recent events, Claude will search the web
  • Always verify time-sensitive information

Not a Search Engine

  • Claude reasons and analyzes rather than just retrieving information
  • Best for understanding and synthesis, not just facts

Cannot:

  • Access your personal accounts (unless you provide access via tools)
  • Remember conversations across different chat sessions
  • Learn or update from your conversations

Accuracy Note

Important

  • Very reliable but not infallible
  • Always verify critical information
  • Especially important for medical, legal, or financial advice
