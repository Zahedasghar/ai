\section{Introduction}\label{introduction}

This guide provides structured prompts for professionals in policy
analysis, economic research, data science, and public administration.
Each prompt follows best practices for effective AI interaction.

\subsection{How to Use This Guide}\label{how-to-use-this-guide}

\begin{enumerate}
\def\labelenumi{\arabic{enumi}.}
\tightlist
\item
  \textbf{Select} the category relevant to your task
\item
  \textbf{Customize} the prompt with your specific details
\item
  \textbf{Iterate} based on results
\item
  \textbf{Refine} for better outputs
\end{enumerate}

``Explain fiscal policy and give me some insights for Pakistan.''

\textbf{Problems:} - Too broad - Vague expectations - No context -
Undefined output format

\subsubsection{Audience-Specific Policy
Summaries}\label{audience-specific-policy-summaries}

\subsection{Secretaries/Senior Bureaucrats}

Extract the top execution risks and 5 most urgent administrative actions
to operationalize the Punjab Climate Change Policy in the next 6 months.

\subsection{Students (Policy \& Governance)}

Summarize the Punjab Climate Change Policy in simple language, identify
main policy tools, and explain how it affects citizens, farmers, and
cities.

\subsection{Journalists/Media}

Provide a neutral, fact-based media brief: key objectives, priority
actions, expected benefits, and accountability mechanisms.

\subsection{Parliamentarians}

Translate the policy into 10 talking points focusing on public benefits,
district-level delivery, and budget oversight.

\subsection{Local Government Officials}

Extract district-level responsibilities and list top 5 actions each
district and tehsil should take immediately.

\subsection{Farmers}

Explain agriculture and water-related parts in simple Urdu and list 5
ways farmers will benefit.

\subsection{Civil Society/NGOs}

Identify how civil society can support awareness, community
preparedness, tree campaigns, and climate-smart livelihood programs.
