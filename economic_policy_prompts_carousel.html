Economic Policy Prompts — General vs Structured

LinkedIn Carousel / Slide Deck

1. Fiscal consolidation

General prompt
“Tell me how Pakistan can reduce its fiscal deficit.”

Structured prompt (right way)

<role>Senior economic policy advisor</role>
<task>Propose 3 fiscal consolidation measures that safeguard growth and the poor</task>
<context>High interest bill, low tax base, IMF targets, election constraints</context>
<output_format>- Measure: [action + fiscal yield]
- Implementation: [steps, timeline]
- Distributional impact: [winners/losers + mitigation]</output_format>
