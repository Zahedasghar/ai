Workshop Overview

Target Audience

Public Policy Officers, Decision Makers, and Government Officials from Punjab Government

This intensive workshop introduces Punjab Government officials to cutting-edge Generative AI and Agentic AI technologies that can deliver exponential productivity gains in public policy work. Participants will learn to leverage AI agents, automated workflows, and advanced prompt engineering to transform how government operatesâ€”moving from traditional manual processes to AI-amplified decision-making systems.

The workshop emphasizes practical implementation of AI systems that can analyze complex policy scenarios, generate comprehensive reports in minutes, and create intelligent workflows that operate autonomously to support governance objectives.

Workshop Duration

Full Day Intensive Workshop - Approximately 8 hours including breaks
