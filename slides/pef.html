    Workshop Overview

    Join us for an engaging three-part workshop where we’ll introduce students, teachers, and researchers to the basics of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and how it can be used in education, research, and everyday life. We’ll focus on practical ways to use AI tools to enhance learning, simplify research tasks, and improve teaching methods. You’ll explore various AI platforms that assist with data analysis, literature reviews, content creation, and making complex tasks easier.

    You’ll get hands-on experience with Generative AI, learning how it can automate tasks like writing academic papers, analyzing data, conducting literature reviews, and supporting innovative research projects. Through interactive exercises and real-life examples, you’ll learn how to effectively use AI tools in your studies or work.

    Key Learning Objectives

    1. Understanding AI in Education and Research
      • Learn the basics of AI and its role in education and research.
      • See how AI helps in content creation, data analysis, literature reviews, and promoting innovation.
    2. AI for Students: Enhancing Learning
      • Discover how students can use AI tools for writing assignments, analyzing data, conducting literature reviews, and understanding complex topics.
    3. AI for Teachers: Improving Teaching and Content Creation
      • Find out how educators can use AI to design engaging lessons, assess student performance, create educational content, and assist with literature reviews.
    4. AI for Researchers: Streamlining Research
      • Learn how to use AI for literature reviews, data collection and analysis, and writing reports to make research more efficient and accurate.
    5. The Impact of AI on Society
      • Understand how AI is changing society and how educational institutions can prepare the next generation to use AI responsibly.

    Workshop Outcomes

    By the end of the workshop, you will:

    • Understand how AI can improve learning, teaching, and research, especially in literature reviews and data analytics.
    • Gain practical experience using AI tools for creating content, conducting literature reviews, analyzing data, and managing research projects.
    • Be prepared to promote responsible and innovative use of AI in educational and research settings.
